About Us


About Hope


Hope blood bank enforces Drug & Cosmetic Act, National blood policy standards and guidelines ensuring proper collection & donation, effective management and monitoring the quality and quantity of the donated blood.

Blood donation is an extremely noble deed, yet there is a scarcity of regular donors across India. We focus on creating and expanding a virtual army of blood donating volunteers who could be searched and contacted by family / care givers of a patient in times of need.
